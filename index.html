About Me

I am a Graphics Researcher at Korea University, passionate about creating intelligent motion synthesis systems.

My research focuses on developing novel algorithms at the intersection of computer graphics, natural language processing, and deep learning. I am particularly interested in text-to-motion synthesis, virtual agent behavior, and neural animation systems.
